WHAT IS PRICE OPTIMIZATION?

We know how hard all of our hosts work to make their spaces great for our guests. We want to help take the guesswork out of pricing your listing, and make it easier to secure more bookings and increase your revenue.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Our smart pricing API takes your listings size, location, amenities and market value into account to generate an optimal price for your unit.

CAN I STILL SET MY OWN PRICES?

Yes! You will be able to set a base price for your listing, so the price will never go under that value.
